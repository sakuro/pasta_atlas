About

What is ?

is a web application for uploading and browsing world maps captured by the mod. Upload a tile set directly from your browser and share it with others, or explore maps that the community has shared.

Features

  • Upload output (tile images and metadata) directly from your browser
  • Browse maps shared by the community
  • Interactive viewer with pan, zoom, surface switching, generation history, train station overlay, and resource tag overlays
  • Sign in with , , or

Built with

  • Backend: Ruby · Hanami 2 · Dry.rb · ROM.rb · PostgreSQL
  • Frontend: SolidJS · Leaflet · Bulma · TypeScript
  • Infrastructure: AWS S3 · CloudFront · ECS Fargate
