Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you sign in via OAuth (, , or ), we receive the following information from the provider:

  • A unique identifier associated with your account on that provider
  • Your username on that provider, used as a suggested username for
  • Your profile picture URL, used as a suggested avatar

We also store information you provide while using the service:

  • Map tile images and metadata you upload ( output) — including map seed, map exchange string, installed mods, game version, and ticks played
  • Your avatar image, if you choose to set one
  • Your display name, timezone, and language preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to provide the service:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To display and share the maps you upload
  • To show your profile to other users

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to operate the service (see Data Storage below).

Cookies

We use a single session cookie (pasta_atlas.session) to maintain your login state and language preference. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

Data Storage

Map images and avatars are stored on Amazon S3 and served via Amazon CloudFront. No other third parties have access to your data.

Data Retention

  • Maps and associated data are retained until you delete them or your account.
  • Uploads from guest accounts are automatically deleted after approximately one week.
  • When you delete your account, your maps and avatar are permanently removed.
